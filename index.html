Renaissance is an opportunity, a privilege, and an honor bestowed on every student. Following the meaning of the very word, 'RENAISSANCE' aims at the 'Rebirth' of the inventive fever and offbeat ideas among the students to encourage them to seek more and more. It is the biggest National Techno/Cultural fest that provides a big platform for all students to learn a lot and become multidimensional. The JECRC Foundation celebrates its annual techno-cultural fest to provide students with a platform to learn and explore beyond the visible limits. This fest acts as a workshop, where students learn qualities like coordinating, organizing, pursuing, managing and celebrating, which are the qualities of entrepreneurs and leaders. This year the extravagance and the thrill will continue for three consecutive days bringing to life the vision of a perfect summer of 2022 with the colors and waves and everything it stands for. Renaissance 2022 explores the theme of "Summer Splash" which aims at blending the spirit of summer with driving our minds towards the core technical concepts, thereby giving birth to one of the biggest techno-cultural events in Rajasthan.

Jaipur Engineering College & Research Centre (JECRC) Foundation, through its constant endeavour, to deliver industry-driven education has been successful in establishing a name for itself in the education sector of the country. Engineering education provided in JECRC is both theoretical and practical resulting in making the student body the apt fit for the companies. Along with education, this programme offers extensive exposure to extracurricular activities at both the national and international levels. With various campaigns and inter-college festivals, the environment on the college premises is forever vibrant and filled with energy providing the students with an experience of a lifetime. JECRC Foundation never fails to provide their students with state-of-the-art infrastructure and technology. It's outstanding placements have always been a key strength.In the matters of academic and technical excellence, JECRC foundation have a long and glorious history. Over the last 18 years, it has emerged as one of the most renowned names in top educational institutions, attracting students from all parts of India.
